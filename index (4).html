GESTURE GUIDE
☝️
INDEX FINGER HEIGHT High hand → high pitch. Low hand → bass notes. Quantized to C major pentatonic scale.
🤏
THUMB + INDEX PINCH Bring fingertips together to trigger a note. Open to release. Glides between pitches while held.
↔️
WRIST HORIZONTAL POS. Left side = dark/muffled (low-pass filter). Right side = bright, open sound.
💡
TIPS Good lighting helps tracking. Keep hand 30–60 cm from camera. One hand only.
